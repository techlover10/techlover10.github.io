During my sophomore year, I was inspired by the "weeder" computer science classes I was enrolled in to write a couple of songs, in an effort to find humor in what are normally considered two of the most taxing courses in Cornell's CS curriculum. C to Assembly (Cache Race Anthem) was inspired by CS 3410, a low-level system organization class. flOCaml was inspired by CS 3110, the OCaml language, and the love-hate relationship every functional programming student experiences.
