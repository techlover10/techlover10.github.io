I run a few musical "projects" in my spare time: rangers.beats is my hip-hop/rap composition project, including original songs and instrumentals. swaggernaut812 is my custom theme music business, exclusively offered through Fiverr.com.

During my sophomore year, I was inspired by the "weeder" computer science classes I was enrolled in to write a couple of songs, in an effort to find humor in what are normally considered two of the most taxing courses in Cornell's CS curriculum. C to Assembly (Cache Race Anthem) was inspired by CS 3410, a low-level system organization class. flOCaml was inspired by CS 3110, the OCaml language, and the love-hate relationship every functional programming student experiences.

Since 2012, I have run a small business/side project selling custom instrumental theme music on Fiverr.com. Shortly after I began offering the service, many people requested vocals- in response, I expanded my offerings to include jingles with rap lyrics. Since then, I've made more than 50 sales, achieved Fiverr's Level 2 status, and maintained near-perfect reviews.
